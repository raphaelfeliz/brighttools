# Product Page Updates - Summary

Here is a quick summary of the recent layout and design improvements we made to your product pages.

---

### Introduction: Dedicated Product templates by family (e.g.: bt-brooms)
**Change:** 
Before we used the `mar26` template for all products. We’ll now use 1 per family. Go ahead and test `bt-brooms` ([link: Shopify Editor](https://admin.shopify.com/store/brighttools-au/themes/180366573842/editor?previewPath=%2Fproducts%2Ftest-product%3Fview%3Dbt-brooms)).

### 1. Title Font Boldness
**Change:**
Before, the title was a standard weight. We’ve made it Extra Bold so it stands out immediately when a customer lands on the page. We didn't shrink the text size like originally asked because shorter names like "Japanese Broom" fit perfectly as they are. Go ahead and look at the top of the page to see how it grabs your attention.

### 2. Price Reordering
**Change:**
Before, the price sat below the review stars. We’ve bumped the price up so it sits right under the product title. Go ahead and see how much faster you spot the price now.

### 3. Styled "Add to Cart" Button
**Change:**
Before, our "Add to Cart" button looked different from Shopify's fast checkout buttons (like Shop Pay). We reshaped ours into a larger, taller pill shape so they match perfectly. We intentionally didn't mess with Shopify's buttons because doing so can break their checkout logic. Go ahead and see how unified the buttons look side-by-side.

### 4. Short Description Text & Background
**Change:**
Before, the key features (bullet points) were small. We increased the text and checkmarks to 20px so they are super easy to read. We also added a setting so you can pick the card's background color (it defaults to a soft yellow). Go ahead and try changing the background color in the Shopify editor block settings.

### 5. Layout Alignment & Sizing
**Change:**
Before, the layout was a bit scattered. Now, everything from the title down to the checkout buttons lines up perfectly on the left. On your computer, the yellow highlight card only takes up as much width as the text needs. On your phone, it stretches beautifully edge-to-edge. Go ahead and shrink your browser window to see how it snaps into the mobile layout.

### 6. Star Rating Color (Judge.me Only)
**Change:**
Before, the Judge.me review stars and our custom product cards had hardcoded red stars. We updated them so they automatically use whatever standard star color you pick in your theme settings. We didn't touch any other star widgets on the store. Go ahead and see how the stars now perfectly match your theme.

### 7. Overlap Spacing
**Change:**
Before, the buy buttons were crammed against whatever section came immediately below them (like videos). We added some clean breathing room underneath them. Go ahead and scroll past the buttons to see the new spacing.

### 8. Mobile Typography Tuning
**Change:**
Before, mobile font sizes felt slightly off-balance. We made the product title a tiny bit bigger on phones, and shrunk the yellow card text just a tiny bit so it fits better inside the screen. Desktop sizes didn't change at all. Go ahead and check the page on your phone or mobile simulator.
