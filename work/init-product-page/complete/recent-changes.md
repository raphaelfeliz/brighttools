# Storefront Product Page Updates - Summary of Changes

A summary of recent improvements made to custom product pages (such as the Brooms template) on the storefront. These updates focus on layout neatness, visual hierarchy, readability, and brand alignment.

---

### 1. Title Font Boldness
* **What changed**: The main product title (e.g., "Japanese Broom") has been updated to be much bolder (Extra Bold).
* **Why**: To highlight the product identity clearly when a customer lands on the page, matching our brand's premium typography style.

### 2. Price Reordering
* **What changed**: The product price has been moved up to display directly beneath the product title, and above the customer review stars.
* **Why**: Customers can now see the price immediately upon reading the product name before viewing social proof ratings.

### 3. Styled "Add to Cart" Button
* **What changed**: The Add to Cart button was resized and reshaped (pill-shaped, taller height of 55px, larger text) to perfectly match the design of checkout buttons like Shop Pay.
* **Why**: Provides a more unified, modern, and prominent shopping interaction that encourages customers to add products to their cart.

### 4. Larger Short Description Text
* **What changed**: Increased the text size and checkmark icons of the product highlight points (the key features card) to a larger, highly readable size (20px).
* **Why**: Makes the key value propositions of our products easy to read at a quick glance on both desktop and mobile viewports.

### 5. Layout Alignment (Left-Align)
* **What changed**: The product highlight cards and the checkout buttons have been adjusted to align perfectly to the left edge of the page container, inline with the title and price.
* **Why**: Creates a clean, structured vertical flow that looks neat and professionally designed.

### 6. Star Rating Color
* **What changed**: All customer review stars across the storefront (including the Judge.me widget and collection cards) have been changed from red to yellow.
* **Why**: Aligns the storefront reviews with standard rating styles and the theme editor's official color palette.

### 7. Overlap Spacing Fix
* **What changed**: Added extra spacing below the buy buttons.
* **Why**: Prevents options or payment links from overlapping or clustering with sections directly underneath (such as video blocks).
